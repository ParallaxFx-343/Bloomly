Bloomly - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Your data stays on your device

Bloomly ("the App") is a gratitude journaling and garden app developed by ParallaxFx-343 ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your choices regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Local data only. Bloomly stores all your data locally on your device using an on-device database (SQLite). We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

The data stored locally on your device includes:

  • Daily gratitude entries (categories selected and optional notes you write)
  • Plant records (type, planting date, growth stage)
  • App preferences (theme, sound settings)
  • Streak and milestone progress

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. User Accounts

Bloomly does not require user accounts, registration, or login. The App works entirely offline and does not sync data to any cloud service.

3. Third-Party Services

Bloomly integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

Google AdMob — We display optional rewarded ads through Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data in accordance with their privacy policy. You can review Google's Privacy Policy.

RevenueCat — In-app purchases and premium subscriptions are processed through RevenueCat. Purchase transactions are handled by Google Play and RevenueCat in accordance with their respective privacy policies. You can review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

These third-party services may collect:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • App interaction data related to ads
  • Purchase and transaction information (processed by Google Play)

4. Data Storage and Security

All your journal entries, plants, and preferences are stored exclusively on your device. If you uninstall the App, all local data will be permanently deleted. We do not have access to your data at any time.

5. Children's Privacy

Bloomly is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App does not require any personal data to function.

6. Your Choices

  • Ad tracking: You can limit ad personalization through your device's settings (Settings > Google > Ads on Android).
  • Data deletion: You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing its data through your device settings.
  • Premium: Purchasing premium removes rewarded ads entirely.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
